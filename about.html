about

We write dreamy, pixel-soft songs that live between shoegaze and internet nostalgia.

Dodo Museum started in 2023 as late-night sketches and voice notes shared between London and Hong Kong. We like hazy reverbs, whispered vocals and tiny sounds that feel like memory. On stage we keep things fragile and close — just enough to float. If you’re here, thanks for listening and for keeping the pixels glowing.
